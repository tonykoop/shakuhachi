# Resources

## Cultural And Build Context

The packet documents a contemporary workshop adaptation of the shakuhachi. It
should name traditional practice respectfully while making clear that a solid
hardwood or PVC proof body is not a faithful madake bamboo reproduction.

## Sources To Confirm Before L3

- Traditional 1.8 shaku D4 dimensional references.
- Utaguchi angle, depth, and insert geometry from measured examples.
- Kinko-ryu and Tozan-ryu fingering/tuning references for public-facing text.
- Bamboo-vs-hardwood bore correction observations from the first prototype.

## Related Repos

- `tonykoop/flutes` for wind-instrument validation habits. NAF K2 corrections
  are not portable to shakuhachi.
- `tonykoop/fujara` for long open-pipe bore and trimming discipline.
- `tonykoop/transverse-flute` for slip-cast/open-pipe documentation patterns.
