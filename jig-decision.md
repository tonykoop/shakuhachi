# Jig Decision

| Operation | Jig choice | Reason | Release check |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Deep bore | Headstock-driven drilling setup | Long D4 body needs aligned bore support | Bore exits within tolerance and stays burn-free |
| Utaguchi cut | Angle block plus hand-file finish | Utaguchi geometry controls response | Coupon or scrap cut reviewed before body |
| Hole drilling | V-block with centerline and thumb-hole reference | Keeps five-hole pattern aligned around the body | Hole stations match drawing before enlargement |
| Bore finishing | Reamer/sanding mandrel | Smooth bore response matters more than surface shine | Bore is smooth and diameter is logged |
| Prototype material choice | PVC or hardwood proof before bamboo claim | Separates acoustic validation from traditional material complexity | Validation row identifies actual material |
